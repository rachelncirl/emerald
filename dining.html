Dining

The 5 Star Emerald Hotel's Panorama Restaurant has one of the most enviable views in Ireland with vistas of the Lakes of Killarney, mountains and gardens stretching before you as you dine. Highlighting Irish and international cuisine, the pinnacle of fine dining in Killarney can be found at The Panorama Restaurant.

Panorama Restaurant

For gourmet food lovers or if you’re looking for the county’s best produce, your farm to fork experience at the hotel’s award-winning Panorama Restaurant will wow you. The restaurant is popular with guests and locals a true seal of approval and highlights regional Irish ingredients including the famed Kerry lamb and beef from the hotel's own farm.

Breakfast is served in the Panorama Restaurant daily from 7.00am - 10.30am daily

Open for dinner from 7-9.30pm on Monday to Saturday inclusive from mid March to end of October. Also open for Dinner on Sundays in July, August and on Sundays of Bank Holiday Weekends (Some exceptions may apply, subject to change)

Bistro

Open daily from 11am to 11pm, serving a wide ranging menu which includes everything from light snacks to succulent flame-grilled steaks cooked on an open grill in front of you by our award winning chefs. The popular terrace is also open during warmer months, for relaxed, al fresco dining.

The Bistro operates on a casual, walk-in basis and does not take reservations. During busy times, please contact the Brasserie Host/ess so that you may be included on the waiting list and contacted when a table becomes available.

Mac's Bar

Mac’s, is our cosy bar and an inviting haven of welcome and warmth. Guests can enjoy conversations with a refreshing beverage or light bites, whilst relaxing by the crackling fire.

Our bar is both charming and relaxing, where dark wood panelling and deep leather arm chairs go hand in hand with open fires & traditional music. Our Killarney hotels experienced staff who have been with us for 40 years, will look after your every need, from a simple pint of the "black stuff" to local advice on your next day's outing. Somewhere to relax and reflect on your day's activities.
