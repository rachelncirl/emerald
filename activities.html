Are you Ready for an Unforgetable Stay

The Emerald Hotel is bursting with some many activities, bordem is impossible. Assides from our award winning Resturants, Spa & Leisure Centre, theres still so much to explore! Active or Relaxed, Young or Old, solo or as a group! There's someting for everyone!

Paint Balling

A day not to be forgotten, for the trill seekers, sharp shooters and tacticians : Langan Paintball course is one not to miss!


Situated in the grounds of Langan Wood, the three courses boast woodland, quarry, marshland & urban maps to keep even the most seasoned paintballer entertained. With a bonus target shooting range and a whole host of game options. This course has been gaining a reputation nationwide. Recently hosting the first Westland Derby Anual Paintballing Half Summer Blitz.


Suitable for a range of ages and experience. Junior Options, closed groups for parties, duals between groups or open sessions for solo tag along, there is bound to be an option here for you!

Horse Riding

The whole family from all ages can enjoy our local equestrian centre. The centre offers high quality equestrian experiences catering for all ages and abilities.


The beauty of Ireland's dramatic north west coastline awaits riders of all abilities. Experienced riders get the opportunity to gallop down Langan Strand and splash in the cool clear water of the Atlantic Ocean. Remember it's not called "The Wild Atlantic Way" for nothing! Beginner and novice riders can enjoy a quiet amble in the sand dunes whilst taking in those breathtaking views.


Whether it is the chance to gallop down the beach, improve your riding or have fun at Pony Camp. Rachel and her team will provide you with a memorable enjoyable horse riding experience in a fun, safe and controlled environment.
