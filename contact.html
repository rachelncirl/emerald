Ireland's most Exciting 5 Star experience

The Emerald Hotel is a family-owned property located in the heart of Ireland in the ancient town. Our tastefully decorated 5-Star property encompasses a traditional Irish welcome & a celebrated family atmosphere.

Since 1975, The Emerald Hotel has been recognized as one of the best hotels in the country. We pride ourselves on our customer service excellence and being one of Ireland’s finest Conference & Wedding Venues. We understand that each of our guests have unique needs and requirements, we strive to ensure these are met & fulfilled to an exceptional standard.

Indulge and dine in our Bar & Restaurant. Escape on a journey unknown at our Spa. Dive deep in our 20m pools & enjoy our leisure facilities. Shop Shoda, our unique and quirky concept on a Café. Celebrate your special occasions in our banqueting suites or enjoy our state-of-the-art meeting & conferencing facilities.

A very warm welcome awaits you at the Emerald Hotel, 'your home away from home'.

Getting Here

Drive

The Emerald is just off the M21 , take the first exit along the N300 and you can not miss us! Situated at the heart of the Country. 40 minutes from Dublin, 45 minutes from Cork, 35 minutes from Galway and sitting a stones throw from the Town

Air

The Emerald is just 40 minutes from Dublin International Airport. Over 50 airlines operate from 170 destinations to Dublin Airport, with up to 600 flights per day. Visit the Dublin Airport website for flight details. Airport Hopper is a daily shuttle bus service to and from Dublin Airport. Further information and timetables are available at Airport Hopper.

Train

The Emerald Hotel is accessable by rail from the local Train Station, 10 minutes drive away. The station if serviced by the Regional Irish Rail Line. The hotel offers a pick up & drop off service upon request complimentary of your stay!

Taxi

Taxis based in the nearby town are available. Experience the local hospitality & charm. We have a long standing relationship with our local drivers, so be sure to access at reception if you require a car
